About

Where perception meets control

I work where perception meets control: extracting geometry, motion and intent from cameras and sensors, then closing the loop so robots act on it reliably. My day-to-day spans classical computer vision (calibration, stereo, SLAM) and modern learned systems (Transformers, vision-language-action models) — with a bias toward what runs in real time and degrades gracefully.
